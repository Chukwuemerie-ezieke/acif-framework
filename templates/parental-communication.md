Dear Parent/Guardian,

**Re: Use of Artificial Intelligence Tools in Curriculum Development**

We are writing to inform you about an important step our school is taking to enhance the quality of education while maintaining the highest standards of safety and accuracy.

**What is happening?**

Our teachers are using Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools — such as AI writing assistants — to help create supplementary teaching materials, including lesson notes, practice exercises, and educational explanations. These tools help our teachers produce more diverse and comprehensive learning materials for your children.

**How are we keeping your child safe?**

We have adopted the **AI-Generated Content Integrity Framework (ACIF)**, a rigorous verification system that requires every piece of AI-generated content to pass through multiple quality checks before it reaches any classroom. These checks include:

1. **Factual accuracy verification** — Every fact, date, and figure is checked against trusted educational sources
2. **Age-appropriateness review** — Content is reviewed to ensure it matches your child's developmental stage
3. **Curriculum alignment** — All content is verified against the national curriculum standards set by NERDC
4. **Cultural sensitivity screening** — Content is checked to ensure it is respectful and appropriate for our community

**Protecting your child's data**

We take your child's privacy very seriously. In compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023:

- Your child's personal information (name, grades, medical details) is **never** entered into any AI tool
- AI tools are assessed for data security before we use them
- All AI-generated content is reviewed by qualified teachers before classroom use

**Your rights**

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:
- Request information about which AI tools are used in your child's education
- View the school's AI Content Registry showing approved materials
- Ask questions about our AI content verification process
- Raise concerns with the school's AI Content Officer
